Welcome to the Bay Area Beauty Room! Our goal is to pamper our clients and help them feel the most beautiful they have ever felt! We offer a variety of services to help you unleash your beauty! Services include facials, back treatments, chemical peels, waxing, eyebrow threading, microdermabrasion, and nail services! Our team strives to cater specifically to the needs of each client, with personalized treatments and aftercare advice!

What sets us apart from other salons? We care about the beauty of all living creatures! Therefore all of our products are eco-friendly, vegan, and natural! We utilize the best of the best products for all of our clients! This way we are able to cater to even the most sensitive of skin types. We want everyone to feel their most beautiful, and by utilizing nature’s most beautiful ingredients, we are able to successfully pamper each of our clients!

OUR FACIALS

The Bay Area Beauty Room provides services such as facials which are individually crafted for each and every client. The facials include aromatherapy and oil massages! Skin analysis allows us to examine the unique characteristics of each client’s skin and therefore helps us select the perfect products for our clients.

WAXING
